Hanako kun

Hanako (花子), real name Yugi Amane (柚木普) is a ghost haunting Kamome Academy's old building's third-floor female toilet, third stall.

Yashiro Nene

Yashiro Nene (八尋寧々) is the self-proclaimed heroine of the series. She is a first-year high school student at Kamome Academy's High School Division.

Minamoto Kou

Minamoto Kou (源光) is a student at Kamome Academy's Middle School Division, and a member of a renowned family of exorcists, the Minamoto Clan.

Mitsuba Sousuke

Mitsuba (ミツバ), real name Mitsuba Sousuke (三葉惣助) is a ghost haunting Kamome Academy Middle School's lockers.

Yugi Tsukasa

Yugi Tsukasa (柚木つかさ) is a ghost haunting Kamome Academy. He is the younger twin brother of Yugi Amane. He currently resides at the school's broadcasting club room, with its members Sakura and Natsuhiko.

Nanamine Sakura

Nanamine Sakura (七峰桜) is a 3rd-year student at Kamome Academy's High School division. She made a contract with Tsukasa and is now bound to him. She is a member of the school's broadcasting club.

Hyuuga Natsuhiko

Hyuuga Natsuhiko (日向夏彦) is a 2nd year at Kamome Academy's High School division. He is a member of the school's broadcasting club.

Minamoto Teru

Minamoto Teru (源輝) is a 2nd year student at Kamome Academy's High School Division and the school's Student Council President. He is also a member of a renowned family of exorcists, the Minamoto Clan.

Tsuchigomori

Tsuchigomori (土籠) is a teacher at Kamome Academy. He is secretly a supernatural representing the academy's 5th School Mystery, the 4PM Bookstacks, and also its curator.
